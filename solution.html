Operational Flow

1. Mission Setup

The operator defines mission area, exclusions, and objective priorities before deployment.

2. Autonomous Recon

The vehicle executes planned sweeps while adapting to current, terrain, and detected obstacles.

3. Threat Flagging

Suspicious signatures are flagged and geotagged for operator review and follow-up action.

4. Route Output

Mission outputs provide route guidance and risk markers for downstream maritime decisions.
